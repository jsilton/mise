## Meal Overview

A clean, bright, and satisfying dinner portioned for two adults and one child. A deeply marinated flank steak is seared and sliced over crisp romaine and butter lettuce, finished with a zesty vinaigrette, fresh avocado, radishes, pepitas, and homemade crispy shallots.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Marinate the meat:** Score the flank steak and place it in the marinade at least 2 hours before cooking (or up to 12 hours).
2. **Make the crispy shallots:** Fry the shallots using the cold-oil start method (can be done up to 3 days ahead). Save the shallot-infused oil for cooking the steak.
3. **Prep the salad toppings:** Chop the cucumbers, dice the avocado, and shave the radishes. Wash and tear the lettuces. Keep them cold.
4. **Make the dressing:** Whisk the vinaigrette ingredients together in a small jar.
5. **Sear the steak:** Sauté the flank steak in a smoking-hot cast-iron skillet using the shallot oil (about 4–5 minutes per side).
6. **Rest & Slice:** Let the steak rest for 10 minutes before slicing thinly against the grain.
7. **Toss and Serve:** Dress the greens, arrange the steak and toppings, and scatter the crispy shallots and pepitas on top just before serving.
