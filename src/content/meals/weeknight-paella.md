## Meal Overview

A simplified paella with golden saffron rice, chorizo, shrimp, and the prized crispy bottom (socarrat). One pan, Mediterranean flavors, impressive results. The arugula salad with lemon adds a simple, fresh complement.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Bloom saffron** - steep in warm broth
2. **Cook chorizo** - render fat, set aside
3. **Build rice** - toast in chorizo fat, add saffron broth
4. **Add proteins** - nestle shrimp and chorizo into rice
5. **Cook undisturbed** - for the socarrat to form
6. **Toss salad** - while paella finishes
7. **Serve** - at the table, straight from the pan with lemon wedges

## Notes

- Don't stir once the rice is in  -  that's how socarrat forms
- Listen for the crackling sound that tells you the bottom is crispy
- Bomba rice is traditional, but short-grain works
