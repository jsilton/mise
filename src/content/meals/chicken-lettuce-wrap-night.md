## Meal Overview

Light, crunchy, and packed with flavor  -  seasoned chicken mince served in crisp butter lettuce cups. The smashed cucumber salad rounds it out with cool, sesame-dressed crunch. This is the perfect warm-weather weeknight meal that feels virtuous but doesn't sacrifice flavor.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Prep ingredients** - dice water chestnuts, mince garlic and ginger
2. **Make cucumber salad** - smash, salt, dress
3. **Cook filling** - high heat, break up chicken, add sauce
4. **Wash lettuce** - separate leaves, pat dry
5. **Serve** - filling in a bowl, lettuce cups on a platter, cucumber salad alongside

## Notes

- Butter lettuce or iceberg gives the best cup shape
- Hoisin and sriracha for dipping on the side
- Water chestnuts add the essential crunch factor
