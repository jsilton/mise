## Meal Overview

The Italian-American crowd-pleaser  -  crispy breaded chicken topped with marinara and melted mozzarella, served over pasta. Garlic bread and a simple salad round out the plate. Everyone loves chicken parm night.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Start pasta water** - boil early
2. **Bread and bake chicken** - preheat oven to 425°F, bake 20 minutes
3. **Cook pasta** - while chicken bakes
4. **Top chicken** - sauce and cheese, broil 2-3 minutes
5. **Toast garlic bread** - last 5 minutes in oven
6. **Toss salad** - right before plating
7. **Plate** - pasta with sauce, chicken on top or alongside

## Notes

- Baking instead of frying makes this easier and less messy
- Use fresh mozzarella for the best melt
- Save some pasta water to thin the sauce if needed
