## Meal Overview

Sweet, tangy, and slightly spicy General Tso's chicken made easy in the slow cooker. The sauce gets sticky and caramelized, coating every piece. Garlicky sesame spinach adds a quick, nutritious green element.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Load slow cooker** - chicken, sauce ingredients, 4 hours on low
2. **Start rice** - 20 minutes before serving
3. **Cook spinach** - quick sauté with garlic and sesame, 5 minutes
4. **Thicken sauce** - cornstarch slurry in the last 30 minutes
5. **Serve** - rice in bowls, chicken and sauce, spinach alongside

## Notes

- Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced scallions
- Add dried chili peppers for authentic heat
- Leftovers reheat well for lunch the next day
