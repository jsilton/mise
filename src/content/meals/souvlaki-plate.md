## Meal Overview

A vibrant Greek dinner — marinated chicken skewers with tangy tzatziki, fluffy rice pilaf, and a crisp horiatiki-style salad. This is summer on a plate, bright with lemon, herbs, and olive oil.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Marinate chicken** - at least 30 minutes (overnight is best)
2. **Start rice pilaf** - 25 minutes
3. **Make tzatziki** - grate cucumber, squeeze dry, mix with yogurt
4. **Grill or pan-sear souvlaki** - 3-4 minutes per side
5. **Assemble salad** - tomatoes, cucumber, olives, feta, red onion
6. **Plate** - rice, souvlaki skewers, salad, generous tzatziki

## Notes

- Warm pita on the side for wrapping
- The tzatziki doubles as salad dressing in a pinch
- Lemon wedges for squeezing over everything
