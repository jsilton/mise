## Meal Overview

Meal prep meets weeknight ease  -  pesto-slathered chicken thighs roast on a sheet pan alongside vegetables. Paired with herbed quinoa and roasted sweet potatoes, this is a balanced, satisfying plate that works for dinner tonight and lunches all week.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Preheat oven** to 425°F
2. **Start quinoa** - 20 minutes
3. **Prep sheet pan** - chicken with pesto, sweet potato cubes with oil
4. **Roast** - 20-25 minutes until chicken hits 165°F
5. **Fluff quinoa** - add lemon and herbs
6. **Plate** - quinoa, chicken, sweet potatoes

## Notes

- Use store-bought pesto for ultimate convenience
- Chicken thighs stay juicier than breasts
- Portion into containers for meal prep
