## Meal Overview

Rich, nutty Panang curry with tender chicken in a coconut milk sauce fragrant with kaffir lime and Thai basil. Simpler than green or red curry but arguably more satisfying. Steamed rice soaks up the creamy sauce, and edamame adds a pop of protein.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Start rice** - 20 minutes
2. **Cook curry paste** - bloom in coconut cream
3. **Add chicken** - cook through in the sauce
4. **Add coconut milk** - simmer until thickened
5. **Steam edamame** - 5 minutes
6. **Serve** - rice in bowls, curry ladled over, edamame on the side

## Notes

- Panang curry paste is thicker and nuttier than other Thai curry pastes
- Kaffir lime leaves are the signature flavor — don't skip them
- A squeeze of lime and Thai basil leaves to finish
