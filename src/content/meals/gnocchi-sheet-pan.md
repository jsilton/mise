## Meal Overview

Pillowy gnocchi roasted until crispy on the outside, surrounded by caramelized vegetables  -  all on one sheet pan. A simple arugula salad rounds out the meal with peppery freshness. This is the weeknight hero for when you want comfort with zero effort.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Preheat oven** to 425°F
2. **Toss gnocchi and vegetables** - with olive oil, garlic, seasonings on sheet pan
3. **Roast** - 20-25 minutes, tossing halfway
4. **Toss salad** - while gnocchi roasts
5. **Finish** - fresh parmesan and basil over the gnocchi

## Notes

- Shelf-stable gnocchi (not frozen) gets crispiest
- Cherry tomatoes burst and create a natural sauce
- A drizzle of balsamic glaze or pesto elevates everything
