## Meal Overview

Sweet, sticky, and crispy  -  homemade honey sesame chicken that puts takeout to shame. The sauce is perfectly balanced between sweet honey and savory soy. Charred broccoli adds smoky depth, and fluffy rice catches every drop.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Start rice** - 20 minutes
2. **Prep chicken** - cube, coat in cornstarch
3. **Fry chicken** - until golden and crispy
4. **Make sauce** - honey, soy, garlic, ginger, sesame
5. **Char broccoli** - high heat in a hot pan or under broiler
6. **Toss chicken in sauce** - coat evenly, garnish with sesame seeds
7. **Serve** - rice, chicken, broccoli

## Notes

- The cornstarch coating is essential for crunch that holds up to sauce
- Toast sesame seeds in a dry pan for extra flavor
- Scallions make a beautiful garnish
