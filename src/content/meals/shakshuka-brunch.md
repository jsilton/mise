## Meal Overview

Eggs poached in a spiced tomato sauce — vibrant, warming, and perfect for brunch or a light dinner. The warm pita is essential for scooping, and the fattoush provides crunch and acid. This meal comes together in one skillet.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Build sauce** - onion, garlic, peppers, spices, tomatoes
2. **Simmer** - until sauce thickens, 15 minutes
3. **Warm pita** - in oven or dry pan
4. **Crack eggs** - into wells in the sauce, cover, cook 5-7 minutes
5. **Assemble fattoush** - toast pita chips, toss salad
6. **Serve** - straight from the skillet with pita alongside

## Notes

- The eggs should be just set on top, still runny underneath
- Crumbled feta over the top is a Middle Eastern tradition
- Fresh herbs (cilantro or parsley) and a drizzle of tahini to finish
