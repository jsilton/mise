## Meal Overview

Slow-simmered chili with warm cornbread — the ultimate cold-weather comfort pairing. The longer the chili simmers, the better it gets. Serve with all the fixings and let everyone top their own bowl.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Brown meat** - break up and sear in batches
2. **Build base** - onions, garlic, spices, tomato paste
3. **Simmer** - add beans, tomatoes, broth; low and slow for at least 1 hour
4. **Make cornbread** - batter and bake during the last 30 minutes
5. **Prep toppings** - shredded cheese, sour cream, scallions, jalapeños
6. **Serve** - big bowls, cornbread wedges, toppings on the table

## Notes

- Chili always tastes better the next day
- A splash of cider vinegar at the end brightens everything
- Top with shredded cheddar, sour cream, and sliced scallions
