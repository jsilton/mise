## Meal Overview

Classic Italian-American comfort: pounded chicken thighs in a rich Marsala wine and mushroom sauce, served over creamy mashed potatoes with lemony green beans on the side. Restaurant-quality weeknight dinner.

## The Menu

- **Chicken Marsala** — tender chicken in mushroom Marsala sauce
- **Perfect Mashed Potatoes** — creamy base for the sauce
- **Green Beans with Shallots and Lemon** — bright, fresh contrast

## Quick Timeline

| Time | Task                                          |
| ---- | --------------------------------------------- |
| 0:00 | Start potatoes boiling. Pound chicken.        |
| 0:15 | Sear chicken in batches.                      |
| 0:25 | Cook mushrooms and make sauce. Mash potatoes. |
| 0:35 | Sauté green beans.                            |
| 0:40 | Plate and serve.                              |

## Notes

- Use dry Marsala wine, not sweet
- Pound the chicken thin for quick, even cooking
- The sauce is amazing over the mashed potatoes
