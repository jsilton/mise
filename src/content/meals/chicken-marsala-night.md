## Meal Overview

Classic Italian-American comfort: pounded chicken thighs in a rich Marsala wine and mushroom sauce, served over creamy mashed potatoes with lemony green beans on the side. Restaurant-quality weeknight dinner.

## The Menu

- [Chicken Marsala](/recipes/chicken-marsala) — tender chicken in mushroom Marsala sauce
- [Perfect Mashed Potatoes](/recipes/perfect-mashed-potatoes) — creamy base for the sauce
- [Green Beans with Shallots and Lemon](/recipes/green-beans-with-shallots-and-lemon) — bright, fresh contrast

## Notes

- Use dry Marsala wine, not sweet
- Pound the chicken thin for quick, even cooking
- The sauce is amazing over the mashed potatoes
