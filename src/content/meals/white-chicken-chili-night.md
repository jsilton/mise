## Meal Overview

A lighter, brighter cousin to traditional chili  -  chicken, white beans, green chiles, and a creamy broth that's warming without being heavy. Buttery cornbread on the side is perfect for dunking.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Build base** - sauté onion, garlic, green chiles, spices
2. **Add broth and beans** - simmer with chicken
3. **Make cornbread** - batter and bake during simmer time
4. **Shred chicken** - remove, shred, return to pot
5. **Finish** - cream or cream cheese for richness
6. **Serve** - bowls topped with sour cream, avocado, cilantro, lime

## Notes

- Rotisserie chicken is a great shortcut
- Crushed tortilla chips as a topping add crunch
- Freezes well for future easy dinners
