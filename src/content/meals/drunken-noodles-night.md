## Meal Overview

Wide rice noodles stir-fried with Thai basil, chili, garlic, and a sweet-salty soy sauce glaze. This is bold, spicy, deeply satisfying street food that comes together in minutes once everything is prepped. Edamame on the side adds protein and freshness.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Soak noodles** - wide rice noodles in warm water until pliable
2. **Prep everything** - slice vegetables, mince garlic, mix sauce
3. **Steam edamame** - 5 minutes, set aside
4. **Stir-fry** - high heat, protein first, then vegetables, then noodles and sauce
5. **Finish** - toss in Thai basil leaves at the very end
6. **Serve** - straight from the wok

## Notes

- Fresh wide rice noodles from an Asian market are far superior to dried
- Don't skimp on the Thai basil  -  it makes the dish
- Adjust chili to taste, but it should have some heat
