## Meal Overview

An elegant, health-forward dinner  -  herb-crusted salmon with a bright lemon emulsion, served alongside herbed quinoa and roasted cauliflower. The fennel-citrus salad adds a fresh, anise-scented contrast. This is the kind of meal that feels like a restaurant dinner.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Start quinoa** - 20 minutes, add herbs when done
2. **Roast cauliflower** - 425°F, 20-25 minutes
3. **Cook salmon** - sear or bake, 10-12 minutes
4. **Make emulsion** - lemon, herbs, olive oil
5. **Assemble salad** - shave fennel, segment citrus
6. **Plate** - quinoa bed, salmon with emulsion, cauliflower and salad alongside

## Notes

- The lemon-herb emulsion ties the entire plate together
- Drizzle tahini over the cauliflower before serving
- A glass of dry white wine completes the experience
