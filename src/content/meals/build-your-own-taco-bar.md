## Meal Overview

The ultimate crowd-pleaser for midweek. Set out carnitas, toppings, and let everyone assemble their perfect taco. Everyone gets what they want, and you're not standing over a stove. Perfect for families with different preferences or dietary needs.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Start carnitas** - morning is best, let slow cooker do the work
2. **Prep rice and beans** - about 30 min before dinner
3. **Prep taco bar components** - shred cheese, chop cilantro, slice limes, dice onions, warm tortillas
4. **Set up taco station** - all components in serving dishes, everyone assembles their own

## Notes

- Offer both flour and corn tortillas - everyone has preferences
- Pickled jalapeños and hot sauce on the side for the adventurous
- Leftover carnitas make great burrito bowls, tacos, or nachos the next day
- Kids can help assemble their own - great for engagement
