## Meal Overview

A clean, simple fish dinner with the satisfying crunch of panko-crusted cod. The garlic roasted potatoes provide hearty starch while steamed broccoli keeps it balanced. This is comfort food that doesn't weigh you down.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Preheat oven** to 425°F
2. **Start potatoes** - cube, toss with oil and garlic, roast first (they take longest)
3. **Prep cod** - top with panko mixture
4. **Bake cod** - add to oven when potatoes are halfway done
5. **Steam broccoli** - last 5-7 minutes
6. **Plate** - squeeze lemon over cod before serving

## Notes

- Don't overcook the cod  -  pull at 135°F internal
- A squeeze of lemon ties everything together
- Tartar sauce or lemon aioli on the side is a nice touch
