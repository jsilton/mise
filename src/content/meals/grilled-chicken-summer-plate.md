## Meal Overview

The quintessential summer dinner  -  juicy grilled chicken with a fresh corn and herb salsa, flanked by crispy smashed potatoes and charred zucchini. Everything benefits from the grill or high heat. This is outdoor cooking season at its best.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Boil potatoes** - for smashing, 15 minutes
2. **Prep grill** - high heat, clean grates
3. **Grill chicken** - 6-7 minutes per side
4. **Smash and roast potatoes** - on the grill or in oven
5. **Grill zucchini** - 3-4 minutes per side
6. **Make corn salsa** - grill corn first, then cut and mix
7. **Plate** - chicken with salsa, potatoes and zucchini alongside

## Notes

- Let chicken rest 5 minutes before slicing
- The corn salsa works as a topping and a side
- A squeeze of lime ties everything together
