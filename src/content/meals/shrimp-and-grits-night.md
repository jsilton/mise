## Meal Overview

Southern comfort at its finest  -  creamy, cheesy grits topped with garlicky, buttery shrimp in a quick pan sauce. The charred broccoli provides a smoky, green counterpoint to all that richness. This is a date night meal that feels special but isn't fussy.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Start grits** - low and slow, stirring frequently, 20-25 minutes
2. **Prep shrimp** - peel, devein, pat dry
3. **Roast broccoli** - high heat while grits cook
4. **Cook shrimp** - sear hot and fast, build pan sauce
5. **Finish grits** - stir in butter and cheese at the end
6. **Plate** - grits in bowl, shrimp and sauce on top, broccoli on the side

## Notes

- Stone-ground grits have the best texture and corn flavor
- The shrimp pan sauce: butter, garlic, lemon, a splash of stock
- A dash of hot sauce in the grits is traditional
