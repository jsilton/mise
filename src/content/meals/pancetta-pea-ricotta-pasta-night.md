## Meal Overview

A quick and comforting pasta dinner portioned for two adults and one child. Orecchiette is tossed in a velvety, egg-free sauce of ricotta, Parmesan, sweet peas, and lemon zest, emulsified using starchy pasta cooking water. It is finished with a topping of shatteringly crisp rendered pancetta.

## Cooking Strategy

1. **Render the pancetta:** Start diced pancetta in a cold skillet and cook until crispy. Remove and drain, reserving 1.5 to 2 tablespoons of the rendered fat in the pan (discard excess).
2. **Boil water:** Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil for the orecchiette.
3. **Build the base:** Sauté the shallot and garlic in the hot pancetta fat, then deglaze the pan with white wine or chicken stock.
4. **Cook pasta:** Drop the pasta and boil until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of starchy pasta water right before draining.
5. **Combine & Emulsify:** Toss hot pasta and peas in the skillet. **Remove from heat** completely, then stir in the ricotta, Parmesan, lemon zest, and pasta water. Stir vigorously to emulsify.
6. **Serve:** Top bowls with the crispy pancetta and serve immediately.
