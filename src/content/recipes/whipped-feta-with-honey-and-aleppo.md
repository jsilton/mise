## Chef's Note

This Mediterranean dip transforms crumbly feta into a silky, whipped spread by blending it with cream cheese and olive oil. The key is processing for a full 3-4 minutes until completely smoothâ€”any graininess means you stopped too early. Aleppo pepper provides gentle, fruity heat that won't overwhelm kids, while honey bridges the salty cheese and fresh dill. Make this ahead; it actually improves after a few hours in the fridge as the flavors meld.

## Directions

1. **The Whip:** Combine feta, cream cheese, olive oil, garlic, and lemon juice in a food processor. Blend for **3-4 minutes**, scraping down sides twice, until completely smooth and silky.

2. **The Spread:** Transfer to a shallow serving bowl or plate. Use the back of a spoon to create swirls and valleys.

3. **The Finish:** Drizzle generously with honey and olive oil. Scatter Aleppo pepper and fresh dill over top. Finish with a pinch of flaky salt.

4. **Serve:** Serve at room temperature with warm pita triangles, cucumber spears, and bell pepper strips.
