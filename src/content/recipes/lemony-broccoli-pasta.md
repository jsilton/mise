## Chef's Note

Broccoli and lemon is a classic Italian pairing - the vegetable's slight bitterness needs bright acid to sing. The broccoli cooks in the same pot as the pasta, so its starchy water enriches the sauce. Finish with fresh lemon juice and good olive oil so the citrus and fat bridge the bitter greens and creamy pasta.
## Directions

1.  **Boil:** Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook for 8 minutes.
2.  **Stage 2:** 2 minutes before the pasta is done, add the broccoli florets to the same pot.
3.  **The Mix:** In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, Parmesan, lemon juice, and zest.
4.  **Drain:** **Reserve 1 cup of pasta water**, then drain the pasta and broccoli.
5.  **Bind:** Return pasta and broccoli to the pot. Add the peas and the lemon-Parmesan mixture.
6.  **Emulsify:** Add the reserved pasta water 1/4 cup at a time, tossing vigorously over low heat until the sauce is glossy and the peas are bright.
7.  **Serve:** Season with salt and plenty of black pepper. Top with extra Parmesan.
