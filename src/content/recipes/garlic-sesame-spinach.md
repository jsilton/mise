  - '1 lb Fresh Baby Spinach'
  - '1 tsp Toasted Sesame Oil'
  - '1 tsp Garlic, minced'
  - '1/2 tsp Salt'
  - '1 tsp Toasted Sesame Seeds'
---

## Directions

1.  **Blanch:** Boil a large pot of water. Add spinach and cook for only 30 seconds until wilted.
2.  **Shock:** Drain immediately and plunge into ice-cold water.
3.  **Squeeze:** Squeeze the spinach with your hands until **bone-dry**. (This is critical for texture).
4.  **Season:** Toss the spinach with sesame oil, garlic, salt, and seeds.

- Grilled Steak or Fish
